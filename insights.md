# Key Insights from Twitch Data Analysis

## Top Games
- **League of Legends** dominates viewership by a wide margin.
- Other popular titles include **Dota 2**, **Counter-Strike: Global Offensive**, and **DayZ**.

## Viewer Geography
- The United States has the highest number of League of Legends viewers.
- Other strong regions include Germany, Canada, and the United Kingdom.

## Device Usage
- Most users watch on **site** (desktop), followed by mobile devices.

## Genre Breakdown
- **MOBA** games (League of Legends, Dota 2, Heroes of the Storm) account for the majority of views.
- **FPS** and **Survival** games also have strong followings.

## Chat Engagement
- Some games show significantly higher chat activity relative to viewership, indicating more engaged communities.

## Time-based Patterns
- Viewership tends to peak during evening hours in the US (roughly 6 PM – 11 PM).
